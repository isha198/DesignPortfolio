UX Design

Study Smart

Study Smart is an app that I designed as part of my innovation through design course at the University of Auckland. Using the tool Adobe XD, I prototyped an app that helped university students to stay on task while studying for a test in a course they didn’t enjoy. My app incorporated several features including a study planning session using SMART goals, a study timer, a collaboration space with friends, and a study review session. I was able to gather insights about my user and better understand their needs and desires through prototype testing and later incorporated their feedback throughout each stage of the design iteration.
